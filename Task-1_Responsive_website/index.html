About US

Welcome to Aditya Air Rifle Club, your premier destination for air rifle shooting enthusiasts! Established in 2016 our club brings together individuals of all ages and skill levels who share a passion for precision, focus, and the thrill of marksmanship. At Aditya Air Rifle Club, we believe that shooting is more than just a sport—it's a discipline that fosters concentration, responsibility, and camaraderie. Whether you're a seasoned sharpshooter or a newcomer eager to learn, our club offers a welcoming environment to hone your skills and enjoy the art of air rifle shooting

State-of-the-Art Facilities:

Our shooting range is equipped with the latest technology to ensure a safe and enjoyable experience for all members.

Training and Coaching:

Learn from experienced coaches who provide guidance to improve your technique and build confidence.

Community Events:

Participate in competitions, workshops, and social gatherings that celebrate the spirit of the sport.

Safety First

Safety is our top priority. We follow strict guidelines to ensure a secure environment for all participants.
